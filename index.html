About public speaking

Public speaking is the art of speaking in front of an audience. It helps people share their ideas, thoughts, and messages with others. A good public speaker speaks clearly, confidently, and connects with the listeners. Public speaking is useful in school, college, and professional life. It improves confidence, communication skills, and leadership abilities.With time and effort, anyone can become a good public speaker

Tips for public speaking

  • STAY confident
  • Look at Eyes of public
  • Stay connected to them
  • use your hand while talking
  • You can do it
